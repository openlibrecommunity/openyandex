Classification method
- A Yandex ADMX policy is chromium_inherited when its ADMX name exactly matches a Chromium policy name from the exact 136.0.7103.156 source snapshot, after removing a trailing _recommended suffix.
- A policy is yandex_only when the normalized name is not present in Chromium and is Yandex/Ya-prefixed or otherwise contains Yandex.
- A policy is not_in_chromium_admx when the normalized name is not present in Chromium and is not Yandex-prefixed. This is not automatically proprietary: some of these may be renamed, removed, deprecated, downstream-renamed, or enterprise-only variants.
